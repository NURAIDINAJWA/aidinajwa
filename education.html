My Education Journey





  1. SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN LEREH
    2.

    As a condition of my childhood in Melaka, I received my education in Melaka. When I was seven years old, my parents decided that I should attend Sekolah Kebangsaan Lereh, which is located in the same neighbourhood as my home. It was the best experience I had in primary school, and I have many wonderful memories of my time there, despite the fact that I was only there for a year because I had moved to Kedah.





  2. SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN LUNAS JAYA
    3.

    As a result of my move to Kedah, I am required to register in a new school. For your information, I am moving to Kedah since my parents have decided to move to Kedah for work reasons and also because they want to return to their hometown. From there, I begin to learn the slang of the Kedah people. It was a thrilling experience for me as a child. Not only that, I get new kedah friend and it was very make me happy.





  3. SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN SIDAM KIRI
    4.

    However, when I reach the age of ten, I will be required to transfer to another school. It is due to the fact that my mum is required to be transferred to another district for work reasons. As a result, I must enrol in a new school and meet new people, including new teachers and classmates, in a new environment.






  4. SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN SIONG
    5.

    After finished my primary school, I continued my education to SMKS which is when I am 13 years old until 17 years old. As teenagers, i was embarking on a new adventure in a new environment. There, I had a lot of fun with my studies, my friends, my teachers, and the surrounding environment. I make a lot of memories there, and it's such a great experience that I'll remember it till I'm old and grey.




  5. UiTM MERBOK, SUNGAI PETANI, KEDAH
    6.

    When I reach the age of eighteen, I will have the opportunity to continue my studies in Information Management at UiTM Kedah. I've been here for two and a half years to study. It is such a wonderful experience to study here since I can improve myself in a variety of ways, including soft skills development and many other things. When I was there, I participated in a variety of programmes and met a diverse range of people. It is there that I learn about life as well as academics.
